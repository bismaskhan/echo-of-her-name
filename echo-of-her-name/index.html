About the Book

The Echo of Her Name is a quiet, haunting story about memory, silence, and the truths we avoid. As the past begins to surface, one name continues to echo — through letters, through absence, through lives that were never meant to collide.

Key Themes

  • Memory & identity
  • Silence & secrets
  • Emotional resilience

Quotes

“Some names never leave. They wait.”
“Silence can be louder than truth.”

About the Author

I’m Bisma Saleem, a student and writer who believes stories are meant to be felt before they’re understood. The Echo of Her Name began as a quiet idea and grew into something I couldn’t ignore.
